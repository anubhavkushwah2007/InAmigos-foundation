Who We Are

InAmigos Foundation was established on September 23, 2020, by our Founder & CEO, Mr. Govind Shukla. Headquartered in Chhattisgarh, we began as a localized grassroots initiative and have rapidly scaled our volunteer networks to operate across Haryana, West Bengal, and multiple other states across India.

Our mission is to create meaningful, lasting change by collaborating with communities, leveraging local expertise, and building solutions that address the root causes of poverty and inequality. We operate across six distinct projects targeting education, livelihood, animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and women empowerment.

We are built on solid legal and regulatory foundations to ensure complete transparency and trust with our donors and partners.

Our Six Impact-Driven Projects

Each project targets a unique systemic challenge in Indian society

Project Seva (Humanitarian Relief)

Purpose: To eliminate sudden hunger and clothing insecurity in urban slums, marginalized rural areas, and low-income communities.

How It Works: Our volunteers deliver nutritious, freshly cooked meals and distribute seasonal clothing or clean school uniforms so underprivileged children don't miss school out of shame.

Key Impact Areas: Urban slums, marginalized migrant blocks, and rural communities across our operational regions.

Meals & Clothes Distributed: 50,000+
Communities Reached: 100+
Active Volunteers: 150+

Project Bachpanshala (Child Education)

Purpose: To close structural education gaps for children from low-income or public-school systems, ensuring no child is left behind.

How It Works: We focus on standard academic support paired with critical modern skills, including digital literacy, emotional resilience, and core life skills.

Key Impact Areas: Rural & semi-urban clusters across India, targeting first-generation learners.

Students Supported: 5,000+
Learning Centers Established: 50+
Scholarship Recipients: 500+

Project Udaan (Women Empowerment)

Purpose: To break generational poverty cycles by granting women living in remote areas agency, financial independence, and entrepreneurial skill sets.

How It Works: Providing targeted training in tailoring, handicrafts, digital marketing, and business management, alongside recurring workshops on leadership and health.

Key Impact Areas: Chhattisgarh, Haryana, and West Bengal - Focus on rural women entrepreneurs.

Women Empowered: 900+
Small Businesses Created: 250+
Monthly Income Increase: 200-400%

Project Jeev (Animal Welfare)

Purpose: To offer regular support, rescue care, and safety to stray animals left vulnerable to harsh weather and starvation.

How It Works: Building local feeding systems and setting up makeshift shelters for stray dogs, cats, and cows. We also partner with veterinary clinics for medical care.

Key Impact Areas: Neighborhood stray networks, urban centers, and community peripheries.

Stray Animals Fed Daily: 50+
Animals Rescued & Rehabilitated: 300+
Veterinary Camps Conducted: 20+

Project Prakriti (Environmental Sustainability)

Purpose: To actively counter rapid deforestation, champion eco-friendly farming practices, and drive localized climate action.

How It Works: Gathering massive volunteer bodies for native sapling plantation drives and local waste segregation awareness programs. We focus on community ownership of environmental change.

Key Impact Areas: Localized green belts, eco-zones, and agricultural communities.

Saplings Planted: 20,000+
Tree Survival Rate: 85%+
Farmers in Eco-Programs: 400+

Project Vikas (Skill Development & Employability)

Purpose: Accelerating career paths for young adults and students entering the modern workforce with practical, job-ready skills.

How It Works: Orchestrating professional-grade internships and practical workshops spanning digital marketing, research, data processing, content writing, and social media management.

Key Impact Areas: Pan-India remote & physical skills development for youth aged 18-30.

Young Professionals Trained: 30,000+
Job Placements Achieved: 5,000+
Internship Partners: 100+
